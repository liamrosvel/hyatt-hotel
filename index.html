about hyatt place tijuana

In Hyatt Place Tijuana you will find everything you need when traveling for business or pleasure.

Strategically located on Boulevard Agua Caliente, one of the most important areas of the city and very near to Zona Rio, the financial and gastronomic districts and numerous medical, dental and cosmetic surgery centers, make it the perfect choice for extended stays or a Mexican getaway.
